Privacy Policy for PlayAll

This privacy policy explains how the PlayAll application collects, uses, and protects user information.

Information Collection

The application may automatically collect certain information including device information, IP address, advertising identifiers, and usage statistics.

Advertising

PlayAll uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data according to Google's privacy policy.

Permissions

The application may request permissions such as internet access, network state, notifications, and location services in order to improve functionality and advertisement relevance.

Children's Information

PlayAll does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Users are advised to review this page for any changes.
